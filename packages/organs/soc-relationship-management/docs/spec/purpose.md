# RelationshipManagement Organ — Purpose & Scope Specification
## Organ ID: ORGX-SOC-RELATIONSHIP_MANAGEMENT
## Version: 0.1.0
## Domain: Social and Relationship

### 1. Business Capability Definition
The RelationshipManagement Organ encapsulates the business capability domain for social and relationship operations within the WebWaka platform. This organ represents a stable, bounded domain that coordinates constituent tissues to deliver cohesive relationship management functionality.

### 2. Scope
This organ is responsible for:
- Coordinating RelationshipManagement-specific tissue compositions
- Enforcing domain boundary constraints for social and relationship
- Managing cross-tissue state consistency within the relationship management domain
- Providing offline-first relationship management capabilities with Nigeria-first optimization

### 3. Exclusions
- Does NOT implement UI presentation (delegated to higher layers)
- Does NOT define deployment topology
- Does NOT handle cross-organ orchestration

### 4. Doctrine Compliance
| Doctrine | Enforcement |
|----------|-------------|
| Build Once Use Infinitely | Organ logic is platform-agnostic and reusable |
| Mobile First | All operations optimized for mobile constraints |
| PWA First | Service worker integration points defined |
| Offline First | Full offline queue with sync-on-reconnect |
| Nigeria First | 30s timeout, en-NG locale, NGN currency default |
| Africa First | Multi-region support with Africa-optimized CDN |
| Vendor Neutral AI | No vendor lock-in for AI capabilities |

### 5. Stability Rationale
RelationshipManagement represents a universal business capability within social and relationship that remains stable across organizational contexts and technological paradigms. Domain boundaries are designed for long-term stability.
