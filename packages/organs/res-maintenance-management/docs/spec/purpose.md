# MaintenanceManagement Organ — Purpose & Scope Specification
## Organ ID: ORGX-RES-MAINTENANCE_MANAGEMENT
## Version: 0.1.0
## Domain: Resource and Asset Management

### 1. Business Capability Definition
The MaintenanceManagement Organ encapsulates the business capability domain for resource and asset management operations within the WebWaka platform. This organ represents a stable, bounded domain that coordinates constituent tissues to deliver cohesive maintenance management functionality.

### 2. Scope
This organ is responsible for:
- Coordinating MaintenanceManagement-specific tissue compositions
- Enforcing domain boundary constraints for resource and asset management
- Managing cross-tissue state consistency within the maintenance management domain
- Providing offline-first maintenance management capabilities with Nigeria-first optimization

### 3. Exclusions
- Does NOT implement UI presentation (delegated to higher layers)
- Does NOT define deployment topology
- Does NOT handle cross-organ orchestration

MaintenanceManagement represents a universal business capability within resource and asset management that remains stable across organizational contexts and technological paradigms. Domain boundaries are designed for long-term stability.
