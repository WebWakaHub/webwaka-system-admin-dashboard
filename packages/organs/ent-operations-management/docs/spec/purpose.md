# OperationsManagement Organ — Purpose & Scope Specification
## Organ ID: ORGX-ENT-OPERATIONS_MANAGEMENT
## Version: 0.1.0
## Domain: Enterprise Management

### 1. Business Capability Definition
The OperationsManagement Organ encapsulates the business capability domain for enterprise management operations within the WebWaka platform. This organ represents a stable, bounded domain that coordinates constituent tissues to deliver cohesive operations management functionality.

### 2. Scope
This organ is responsible for:
- Coordinating OperationsManagement-specific tissue compositions
- Enforcing domain boundary constraints for enterprise management
- Managing cross-tissue state consistency within the operations management domain
- Providing offline-first operations management capabilities with Nigeria-first optimization

### 3. Exclusions
- Does NOT implement UI presentation (delegated to higher layers)
- Does NOT define deployment topology
- Does NOT handle cross-organ orchestration

### 4. Doctrine Compliance
| Doctrine | Enforcement |

### 5. Stability Rationale
OperationsManagement represents a universal business capability within enterprise management that remains stable across organizational contexts and technological paradigms. Domain boundaries are designed for long-term stability.
