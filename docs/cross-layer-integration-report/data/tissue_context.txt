TISSUE-UNIVERSE CONTEXT
========================

CONSTITUTION:
- Tissue = cross-category capability assembly (composition of Cells)
- Tissue MAY span multiple categories
- Tissue MUST NOT contain raw organelles, business-domain logic, UI, or bypass Cell abstraction
- 4 Classification Axes: Cross-Functional, Lifecycle, Structural Boundary, Scale & Deployment
