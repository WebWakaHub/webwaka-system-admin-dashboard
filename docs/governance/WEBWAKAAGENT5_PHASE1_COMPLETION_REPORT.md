## Nigeria-First Principles Integration

All 9 Phase 1 documents have been refined with explicit **Field Reality Considerations (MANDATORY)** sections that address:

### Nigeria-First
- Context-specific considerations for the Nigerian market
- Local payment systems and fraud prevention
- Social engineering attacks targeting users with low digital literacy
- Device sharing scenarios and physical device theft

### Africa-First
- Adaptability across diverse African markets
- Variations in regulatory environments and infrastructure
- Diverse risk profiles and attack vectors across the continent
- Limited access to technical support in some regions

### Mobile-First
- Prioritization of mobile device realities
- Low-cost Android devices with limited resources
- Battery drain and memory constraints
- Intermittent connectivity and network resilience

### PWA-First
- Progressive Web App-specific considerations
- Service worker caching and offline capabilities
- Push notifications and home screen installation
- Insecure caching of sensitive data and service worker hijacking

### Offline-First
- Offline operation and data synchronization challenges
- Data consistency across offline and online states
- Conflict resolution in multi-device scenarios
- Secure offline storage and data recovery
