# WebWaka Monetization & Revenue Streams

**Document Type:** Canonical Product Strategy
**Authority:** webwakaagent2 (Product & Platform Strategy)
**Status:** DRAFT
**Version:** 0.1
**Date:** 2026-02-04

## 1. Introduction

This document details the monetization strategy and various revenue streams for the WebWaka platform. Our approach is to diversify revenue sources to ensure long-term financial sustainability while keeping the core platform affordable for our target users.

## 2. Primary Revenue Streams

### 2.1. Subscription Fees

The primary source of revenue will be the monthly subscription fees paid by tenants, as outlined in the *Subscription & Pricing Strategy* document. This provides a predictable and recurring revenue stream.

### 2.2. Transaction Fees

For tenants using the Commerce Suite, we will charge a small transaction fee on each sale processed through the platform. This fee will be a percentage of the transaction value and will be competitive with other payment gateways in the market.

## 3. Secondary Revenue Streams

### 3.1. Marketplace Commissions

For tenants using the Multi-Vendor Marketplace module, we will take a commission on the sales of their vendors. This aligns our success with the success of the marketplaces built on our platform.

### 3.2. Premium Features & Add-ons

We will offer a range of premium features and add-ons that tenants can purchase to extend the functionality of their platform. Examples include advanced analytics, marketing automation tools, and integrations with third-party services.

## 4. Ecosystem Revenue Streams

### 4.1. Partner Revenue Share

As detailed in the *Subscription & Pricing Strategy*, we will have a revenue-sharing model with our partners. While this is a payout, it also incentivizes partners to bring more tenants to the platform, indirectly driving our primary revenue streams.

### 4.2. Marketplace App Store

As the platform matures, we will launch a marketplace app store where third-party developers can sell their own modules and extensions to WebWaka tenants. We will take a commission on all sales through the app store.
