## 1. Introduction

This document provides a detailed definition of the initial focus suites for the WebWaka platform: the Commerce Suite and the Transportation Ecosystem Suite. These suites have been selected as the initial focus for Phase 1 development based on their significant market demand and strategic importance in the African context.

## 2. Commerce Suite

**Objective:** To provide a comprehensive and affordable solution for small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs) in Africa to manage their retail operations, both online and offline.

### 2.1. Core Modules

#### 2.1.1. Point of Sale (POS)

*   **Description:** A mobile-first POS system that enables merchants to process sales, manage inventory, and accept payments in any environment, with or without an internet connection.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   Offline-first functionality
    *   Support for multiple payment methods (cash, mobile money, card)
    *   Real-time inventory tracking
    *   Sales reporting and analytics

#### 2.1.2. Single-Vendor E-commerce

*   **Description:** A simple and intuitive e-commerce solution that allows individual merchants to create their own online store and sell their products directly to customers.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   Easy-to-use store builder
    *   Integrated payment and shipping options
    *   Mobile-responsive design
    *   Customer management tools

#### 2.1.3. Multi-Vendor Marketplace

*   **Description:** A powerful marketplace solution that enables entrepreneurs to create their own multi-vendor e-commerce platforms, similar to Amazon or Jumia.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   Vendor registration and management
    *   Commission and payout management
    *   Product and order management for vendors
    *   Marketplace-wide search and discovery

## 3. Transportation Ecosystem Suite

**Objective:** To provide a flexible and scalable platform for building and managing a wide range of transportation and logistics services, from ride-hailing to last-mile delivery.

### 3.1. Core Modules

#### 3.1.1. Ride-Hailing

*   **Description:** A complete ride-hailing solution that enables entrepreneurs to launch their own Uber-like service.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   Real-time driver and passenger matching
    *   GPS tracking and route optimization
    *   In-app payments and driver payouts
    *   Rating and review system

#### 3.1.2. Delivery Services

*   **Description:** A versatile delivery management solution that can be used for a variety of services, including food delivery, package delivery, and courier services.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   Order management and dispatching
    *   Real-time tracking for customers
    *   Proof of delivery (photo and signature)
    *   Route planning and optimization for drivers

#### 3.1.3. Fleet Management

*   **Description:** A comprehensive fleet management solution that helps businesses track and manage their vehicles and drivers.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   Real-time vehicle tracking
    *   Driver behavior monitoring
    *   Fuel and maintenance management
    *   Trip history and reporting
