## 1. Introduction

This document defines the key performance indicators (KPIs) and success metrics for the WebWaka platform. These metrics will be used to track our progress against our strategic goals, measure the health of our platform and ecosystem, and inform our decision-making.

## 2. Platform Adoption Metrics

*   **Number of Active Tenants:** The total number of tenants who have at least one active user on the platform.
*   **Tenant Growth Rate:** The rate at which new tenants are signing up for the platform.
*   **Tenant Churn Rate:** The rate at which tenants are leaving the platform.

## 3. User Engagement Metrics

*   **Daily Active Users (DAU):** The number of unique users who interact with the platform on a daily basis.
*   **Monthly Active Users (MAU):** The number of unique users who interact with the platform on a monthly basis.
*   **User Retention Rate:** The percentage of users who return to the platform after their first visit.

## 4. Ecosystem & Marketplace Metrics

*   **Number of Active Developers:** The number of developers who are actively building on the WebWaka platform.
*   **Number of Marketplace Apps:** The total number of apps available in the WebWaka Marketplace.
*   **Marketplace App Installs:** The number of times that marketplace apps have been installed by tenants.

## 5. Financial Metrics

*   **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR):** The total amount of recurring revenue that we generate from subscriptions each month.
*   **Average Revenue Per Tenant (ARPT):** The average amount of revenue that we generate from each tenant per month.
*   **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV):** The total amount of revenue that we can expect to generate from a single customer over the course of their relationship with us.
