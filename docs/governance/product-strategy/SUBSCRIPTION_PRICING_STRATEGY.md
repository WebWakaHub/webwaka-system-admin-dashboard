## 1. Introduction

This document outlines the subscription and pricing strategy for the WebWaka platform. Our pricing model is designed to be simple, transparent, and affordable, making the platform accessible to businesses of all sizes in Africa. We will offer a tiered subscription model, with different plans tailored to the needs of different user segments.

## 2. Pricing Principles

*   **Affordability:** Our pricing will be competitive and affordable for African SMBs.
*   **Transparency:** Our pricing will be simple and easy to understand, with no hidden fees.
*   **Value-Based:** Our pricing will be based on the value that our platform provides to our customers.
*   **Scalability:** Our pricing will be designed to scale with our customers as their businesses grow.

## 3. Subscription Tiers

We will offer three subscription tiers for tenants:

### 3.1. Starter Plan

*   **Target Audience:** Individual entrepreneurs and small businesses.
*   **Price:** $10/month
*   **Features:**
    *   1 User Account
    *   Single-Vendor E-commerce
    *   POS
    *   Basic Reporting

### 3.2. Growth Plan

*   **Target Audience:** Growing businesses and startups.
*   **Price:** $50/month
*   **Features:**
    *   5 User Accounts
    *   Multi-Vendor Marketplace
    *   Advanced Reporting
    *   API Access

### 3.3. Enterprise Plan

*   **Target Audience:** Large businesses and enterprises.
*   **Price:** Custom
*   **Features:**
    *   Unlimited User Accounts
    *   All Suites and Modules
    *   Dedicated Support
    *   Custom Integrations

## 4. Partner Pricing

We will offer a separate pricing model for partners, which will be based on a revenue-sharing agreement. Partners will earn a commission on the subscription fees of the tenants they onboard.
