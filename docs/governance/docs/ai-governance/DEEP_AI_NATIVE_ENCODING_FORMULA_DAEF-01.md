## SECTION II — DEFINITION OF DEEP AI-NATIVE ENCODING

**Deep AI-Native Encoding** is the process of symmetrically and horizontally embedding standardized AI augmentation hooks inside every canonical organ. This ensures that the entire biological architecture is AI-augmentable by design, not by exception.

> **AI-native ≠ AI-driven.**
> AI-native means every domain is structurally prepared to be augmented by the cognitive fabric. It does not imply that every function is controlled by AI.

Deep Encoding requires that:

- AI hooks are embedded inside every canonical organ.
- AI extension points are standardized via the **AI Extension Port Standard (AIPS)**.
- AI routing is governed by the **`SYSX-AI`** system.
- AI runtime binding is managed exclusively by the **`RUNTIME-ADAPTER-AI`**.
- AI entitlement enforcement is mandatory for every invocation.
- A complete, unbroken AI audit trail is mandatory.
- AI compensation and rollback discipline is mandatory.
