## SECTION IX — KEY RISKS & CONTRADICTIONS

The forensic scan revealed a highly coherent and internally consistent governance model. However, several potential risks and areas requiring clarification were identified.

- **Undefined Agent Roles:** The roles for `webwakaagent2` and `webwakaagent6` are not defined in the `AGENT_EXECUTION_PROTOCOL.md`. This could represent a gap in the organizational structure or planned future expansion.
- **Incomplete Domain Translation:** 7 of the 18 canonical domains are not yet covered by the Phase II-A translation matrices. The reintegration is incomplete until these are addressed.
- **Circular Dependency Risk:** The governance framework itself is complex. The `WEBWAKA_MASTER_DOCUMENT.md` references other documents, which in turn reference it. While currently stable, any uncoordinated change could create a constitutional deadlock.
- **AGVE as a Single Point of Failure:** The entire system's integrity relies on the AGVE. Any bug, misconfiguration, or failure in the AGVE could either allow constitutional drift or cause an unwarranted platform-wide freeze.
- **Founder Bottleneck:** The Founder (`webwaka007`) is the sole authority for ratification and constitutional changes. This creates a potential bottleneck for high-velocity development and decision-making.
