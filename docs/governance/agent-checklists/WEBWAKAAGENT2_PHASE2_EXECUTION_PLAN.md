## Risks and Mitigation

### Risk 1: Unclear or Changing Requirements
**Impact:** High - Could delay implementation and cause rework  
**Mitigation:** 
- Establish clear requirements definition process
- Implement change control for requirements
- Maintain continuous communication with stakeholders

### Risk 2: Product-Engineering Misalignment
**Impact:** High - Could lead to wrong features being built  
**Mitigation:**
- Daily standups and frequent alignment checks
- Clear acceptance criteria for all features
- Rapid feedback loops and course correction

### Risk 3: Scope Creep
**Impact:** Medium - Could delay Phase 2 completion  
**Mitigation:**
- Strict prioritization framework
- Change control process for new features
- Regular scope reviews with Chief of Staff

### Risk 4: Resource Constraints
**Impact:** Medium - Could limit feature delivery  
**Mitigation:**
- Ruthless prioritization of features
- Phased delivery approach
- Coordination with Engineering on capacity

### Risk 5: Technical Feasibility Issues
**Impact:** Medium - Could require requirements adjustment  
**Mitigation:**
- Early coordination with Architecture
- Feasibility assessment before commitment
- Flexible requirements approach
