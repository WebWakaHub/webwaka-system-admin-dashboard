# Phase & Wave Execution Plan

**Document Type:** Product & Platform Strategy
**Department:** Product & Platform Strategy (webwakaagent2)
**Status:** DRAFT

---

## Introduction

This document outlines the phased and waved execution plan for the WebWaka platform, aligning with the 10-Year Platform Roadmap. It breaks down the long-term vision into actionable stages, ensuring a focused and incremental delivery of value.

## Execution Phases

| Phase | Title | Duration | Primary Focus |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Foundation & Governance | 3 Months | Establish core governance, activate all agent departments, and complete all foundational strategy documents. |
| 2 | Core Platform Implementation | 9 Months | Build and deploy the essential platform services, including identity, multi-tenancy, and payment gateways. |
| 3 | Initial Suite Launch | 12 Months | Launch the Commerce and Transportation suites to a pilot group of users and partners. |
| 4 | Ecosystem & Expansion | 24 Months | Grow the developer ecosystem, launch the public SDK, and begin expansion into new geographical markets. |

## Execution Waves (Within Phases)

Each phase will be executed in a series of waves, allowing for iterative development and feedback.

### Phase 2: Core Platform Implementation Waves

*   **Wave 2.1:** Identity and Access Management (IAM) service.
*   **Wave 2.2:** Multi-Tenancy architecture and Tenant onboarding workflow.
*   **Wave 2.3:** Payment Gateway integration and transaction processing.
*   **Wave 2.4:** Core API gateway and initial monitoring/logging infrastructure.

### Phase 3: Initial Suite Launch Waves

*   **Wave 3.1:** Commerce Suite - Single Vendor Marketplace MVP.
*   **Wave 3.2:** Transportation Suite - Ticketing System MVP.
*   **Wave 3.3:** Commerce Suite - Multi-Vendor Marketplace features.
*   **Wave 3.4:** Partner onboarding and support tools for both suites.
