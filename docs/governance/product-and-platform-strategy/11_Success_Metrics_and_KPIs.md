_# Success Metrics & KPIs

**Document Type:** Product & Platform Strategy
**Department:** Product & Platform Strategy (webwakaagent2)
**Status:** DRAFT

---

## 1. Guiding Principle

"What gets measured gets managed." This document defines the key performance indicators (KPIs) that the Product & Platform Strategy department will use to measure the success of the WebWaka platform. These metrics are designed to provide a comprehensive view of the platform's health, growth, and impact, ensuring that our strategic decisions are data-driven.

## 2. KPI Hierarchy

Our metrics are organized into a hierarchy that flows from our highest-level strategic objectives down to specific product-level performance.

| Level | Focus | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| **Level 1: North Star Metric** | The single most important measure of the platform's overall success and value creation. | Founder / Founder Agent |
| **Level 2: Strategic Pillars** | High-level metrics that track the health of the core pillars of our platform strategy: Growth, Engagement, and Monetization. | Product & Platform Strategy (webwakaagent2) |
| **Level 3: Suite & Product KPIs** | Granular metrics that measure the performance of individual suites, features, and user segments. | Respective Product & Engineering Leads |

## 3. Core Platform KPIs

### Level 1: North Star Metric

- **Total Active Tenants:** The number of unique, active businesses operating on the WebWaka platform across all partners and suites. This is the ultimate measure of our reach and impact.

### Level 2: Strategic Pillar Metrics

| Pillar | KPI | Description | Strategic Importance |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Growth** | **Partner Acquisition Rate:** The rate at which we are onboarding new Partners. | Measures the success of our go-to-market strategy and the appeal of our platform to key distribution channels. |
| | **Tenant Acquisition Rate (via Partners):** The rate at which Partners are successfully onboarding their own customers. | Measures the effectiveness of our Partners and the scalability of our indirect growth model. |
| **Engagement** | **Monthly Active Tenants:** The number of tenants who perform a core action within a given month. | Indicates the ongoing value the platform is providing to businesses. |
| | **Gross Merchandise Volume (GMV):** The total value of all transactions processed through the platform. | The primary measure of the economic activity being enabled by WebWaka. |
| **Monetization** | **Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR):** The total annualized value of our recurring revenue streams (Platform Licensing Fees). | Measures the stability and predictability of our business model. |
| | **Average Revenue Per Tenant (ARPT):** The average revenue generated per tenant, including both recurring and consumption-based fees. | Tracks our ability to increase value and monetization over time. |

## 4. Reporting & Cadence

These KPIs will be tracked and reported on a regular basis. A centralized dashboard will be created and maintained by the Data, Analytics & Intelligence department (webwakaagent8). The Product & Platform Strategy department will review these metrics weekly to inform roadmap prioritization and strategic adjustments.
