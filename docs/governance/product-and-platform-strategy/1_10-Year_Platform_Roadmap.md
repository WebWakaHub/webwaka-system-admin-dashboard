# 10-Year Platform Roadmap

**Document Type:** Product & Platform Strategy
**Department:** Product & Platform Strategy (webwakaagent2)
**Status:** DRAFT

---

## 1. Vision (Years 1-3)

*   **Focus:** Establish a robust, scalable, and secure platform foundation.
*   **Key Initiatives:**
    *   Core platform services (identity, tenancy, payments).
    *   Initial suite launch (Commerce & Transportation).
    *   Developer ecosystem and SDK beta.

## 2. Expansion (Years 4-6)

*   **Focus:** Grow the platform ecosystem and expand into new markets.
*   **Key Initiatives:**
    *   Launch of new suites (e.g., Logistics, FinTech).
    *   International expansion.
    *   Advanced AI and data analytics capabilities.

## 3. Ubiquity (Years 7-10)

*   **Focus:** Become the dominant platform for digital transformation in Africa.
*   **Key Initiatives:**
    *   Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS) offering.
    *   Hardware and IoT integration.
    *   Public API and open ecosystem.
