_# Subscription & Pricing Strategy

**Document Type:** Product & Platform Strategy
**Department:** Product & Platform Strategy (webwakaagent2)
**Status:** DRAFT

## 1. Guiding Principles

Our subscription and pricing strategy is designed to be simple, transparent, and aligned with our partners' success. The core principles are:

- **Value-Based:** Pricing should reflect the value and capabilities unlocked by the platform.
- **Scalable:** The model must accommodate a wide range of customers, from individual entrepreneurs to large enterprises.
- **Partner-Centric:** Our primary customers are our Partners (e.g., banks, telcos). The model empowers them to create their own pricing for their end-customers (Tenants).
- **Predictable:** Partners should have clear, predictable costs to build a sustainable business on our platform.

## 2. Platform Pricing Model (WebWaka to Partner)

WebWaka will charge Partners based on a two-tiered model: a recurring platform access fee and a consumption-based fee.

| Fee Type | Description | Metric |
|---|---|---|
| **Platform Access Fee** | A recurring monthly or annual fee for access to the WebWaka platform and a specific set of Suites (e.g., Commerce, Transportation). | Fixed fee per Partner, potentially tiered by the number of included Suites or support level. |
| **Consumption Fee** | A usage-based fee tied to the volume of activity generated by the Partner's Tenants. | This will be based on key metrics like number of active Tenants, total transaction volume (GMV), or number of API calls. |

## 3. Partner Pricing Model (Partner to Tenant)

Partners have the flexibility to define their own pricing models for their Tenants. This allows them to tailor their offerings to their specific market and customer base. WebWaka will provide a flexible billing engine to support various models that Partners can implement.

**Supported Tenant Pricing Models:**

- **Tiered Subscriptions:** Partners can create multiple subscription tiers (e.g., Basic, Pro, Enterprise) for their Tenants, with different feature sets and usage limits.
- **Per-User Fees:** Charge based on the number of users within a Tenant's organization.
- **Transaction Fees:** Take a percentage or a fixed fee from each transaction processed by the Tenant.
- **Hybrid Models:** Combine elements of the above models.

## 4. Strategic Rationale

This two-level pricing strategy allows WebWaka to maintain a predictable revenue stream through platform access fees while also sharing in the success of our Partners through consumption-based fees. By giving Partners control over Tenant pricing, we empower them to act as true resellers and innovators, fostering a vibrant and competitive ecosystem on top of the WebWaka platform. This aligns our financial success directly with the value we create for our entire user hierarchy.
