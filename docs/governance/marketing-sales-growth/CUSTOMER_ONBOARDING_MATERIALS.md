## 1. Onboarding Philosophy

Our customer onboarding process is designed to be a seamless transition from the sales process to long-term success on the WebWaka platform. Our goal is to empower our customers to achieve their business objectives as quickly and efficiently as possible. We believe that a successful onboarding experience is the foundation of a strong, long-lasting customer relationship.

## 2. The Onboarding Journey

The onboarding journey is a structured process that guides customers from the moment they sign their contract to the successful launch of their platform.

| Phase | Description | Key Activities | Key Deliverables |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. Welcome & Kick-off** | Initial engagement with the customer to set expectations and plan the onboarding process. | Welcome email, kick-off call, project plan creation. | A detailed project plan with timelines, milestones, and key stakeholders. |
| **2. Technical Setup & Training** | Configuring the customer's environment and providing technical training to their team. | Environment provisioning, technical training workshops, developer sandbox setup. | A fully configured and accessible WebWaka environment. |
| **3. Solution Implementation** | Guiding the customer through the process of building and launching their solution. | Regular check-in calls, technical support, best practice guidance. | A successfully launched MVP on the WebWaka platform. |
| **4. Go-Live & Handover** | Supporting the customer through their go-live process and transitioning them to ongoing support. | Go-live readiness check, post-launch review, handover to the customer success team. | A smooth and successful go-live and a clear plan for ongoing support. |

## 3. Onboarding Materials

### 3.1. Welcome Kit

*   **Welcome Email:** A personalized email from their dedicated Customer Success Manager, welcoming them to WebWaka and outlining the next steps.
*   **Onboarding Guide:** A comprehensive guide that walks them through the onboarding process, introduces them to their onboarding team, and provides links to key resources.
*   **Platform Access:** Clear instructions on how to access their WebWaka environment and developer sandbox.

### 3.2. Training Materials

*   **Technical Documentation:** Access to our comprehensive technical documentation, including API references, SDK guides, and tutorials.
*   **Training Workshops:** A series of live, interactive workshops covering key aspects of the WebWaka platform, from architecture to application development.
*   **Video Tutorials:** A library of on-demand video tutorials that cover common use cases and technical tasks.

### 3.3. Implementation Resources

*   **Best Practice Guides:** A collection of guides that provide best practices for building and launching successful platforms on WebWaka.
*   **Code Samples & Templates:** A library of code samples and templates to accelerate development.
*   **Dedicated Support:** Access to our dedicated technical support team to help them overcome any challenges they may encounter.
