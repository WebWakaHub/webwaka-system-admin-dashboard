## 1. Program Vision

The WebWaka Partner & Affiliate Program is a core pillar of our growth strategy. Our vision is to build a thriving ecosystem of partners who are empowered to build, sell, and service solutions on the WebWaka platform. We succeed when our partners succeed.

## 2. Program Tiers

Our program is structured in three tiers to accommodate different levels of partner engagement and expertise.

| Tier | Description | Target Audience | Benefits |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. Affiliate Partners** | Refer new customers to WebWaka and earn a commission on referred sales. | Bloggers, influencers, and industry consultants. | Referral commissions, marketing materials, affiliate dashboard. |
| **2. Solution Partners** | Build and implement solutions for clients on the WebWaka platform. | Development agencies, system integrators, and technical consultants. | All affiliate benefits, plus technical training, dedicated support, and co-marketing opportunities. |
| **3. Strategic Partners** | Collaborate with WebWaka on joint go-to-market initiatives and product integrations. | Technology companies, industry associations, and large enterprises. | All solution partner benefits, plus joint business planning, dedicated alliance manager, and access to our product roadmap. |

## 3. Affiliate Program Details

*   **Commission:** Affiliates will earn a recurring commission of 20% on all subscription revenue for the first 24 months of a referred customer's life.
*   **Cookie Window:** 90 days.
*   **Payouts:** Monthly payouts via PayPal or bank transfer.
*   **Requirements:** Affiliates must have an established audience and a commitment to promoting WebWaka in an ethical and brand-aligned manner.

## 4. Solution Partner Program Details

*   **Revenue Share:** Solution Partners will earn a 30% revenue share on the subscription fees of clients they bring to the platform, in addition to their own service fees.
*   **Training & Certification:** We will provide a comprehensive training and certification program to ensure our partners are experts on the WebWaka platform.
*   **Co-marketing:** We will collaborate with our Solution Partners on webinars, case studies, and other marketing initiatives to drive new business for both parties.
*   **Requirements:** Solution Partners must have a proven track record of delivering high-quality technical solutions and a dedicated team of certified WebWaka developers.

## 5. Strategic Partner Program Details

*   **Custom Agreements:** Strategic partnerships will be governed by custom agreements that outline the specific terms of the collaboration, including revenue sharing, co-investment, and intellectual property rights.
*   **Joint Go-to-Market:** We will work with our Strategic Partners to develop and execute joint go-to-market plans that target specific industries or customer segments.
*   **Product Integration:** We will provide dedicated technical resources to support the integration of our Strategic Partners' products with the WebWaka platform.
*   **Requirements:** Strategic Partners must be established leaders in their respective markets and have a clear vision for how a partnership with WebWaka will create mutual value.
