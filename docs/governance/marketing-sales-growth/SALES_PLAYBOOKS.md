## 1. Introduction to the WebWaka Sales Process

This document provides a playbook for the WebWaka sales team. Our sales process is consultative and value-driven. We are not just selling a product; we are helping our clients build the future of their businesses. Our role is to understand their vision, identify their challenges, and demonstrate how WebWaka is the superior foundation for their success.

## 2. The Sales Funnel

Our sales funnel is designed to qualify leads and move them through a structured process of discovery, evaluation, and decision-making.

| Stage | Description | Key Activities | Exit Criteria |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. Lead Qualification** | Initial screening of inbound and outbound leads. | Lead scoring, initial contact, BANT (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline) assessment. | Lead is qualified as a Sales Qualified Lead (SQL). |
| **2. Discovery** | Deep dive into the prospect's business, goals, and challenges. | Needs analysis, stakeholder mapping, technical discovery. | Mutual understanding of the problem and the potential for a solution. |
| **3. Solutioning** | Presenting a tailored WebWaka solution. | Product demo, solution proposal, technical deep dive. | Prospect agrees that WebWaka is a viable solution. |
| **4. Proposal & Negotiation** | Presenting a formal proposal and negotiating terms. | Pricing proposal, contract negotiation, legal review. | Verbal agreement to move forward. |
| **5. Closed Won** | Finalizing the contract and handing off to the onboarding team. | Contract signed, initial onboarding meeting scheduled. | The deal is officially closed. |

## 3. Playbook by Target Audience

### 3.1. Playbook: Platform Entrepreneurs

*   **Focus:** Speed to market, de-risking development, and scaling vision.
*   **Discovery Questions:**
    *   "Tell me about the platform you want to build. What is the big vision?"
    *   "What are the biggest technical hurdles you anticipate?"
    *   "How are you currently planning to build this? What is your timeline?"
*   **Objection Handling:**
    *   *"This seems too complex for my needs."* -> "We handle the complexity so you can focus on your business. Let's start with the core primitives you need for your MVP."
    *   *"I can build this myself with open-source tools."* -> "You could, but at what cost? WebWaka provides an enterprise-grade, resilient foundation out of the box, saving you years of development and maintenance."

### 3.2. Playbook: Established Enterprises

*   **Focus:** Digital transformation, unlocking new revenue streams, and future-proofing the business.
*   **Discovery Questions:**
    *   "What are the key strategic initiatives for your business over the next 1-3 years?"
    *   "How are your current systems limiting your ability to innovate and compete?"
    *   "What is the potential revenue impact of launching a new digital platform?"
*   **Objection Handling:**
    *   *"We have a lot of legacy systems."* -> "WebWaka is designed to integrate with your existing infrastructure, allowing you to modernize incrementally without a massive rip-and-replace project."
    *   *"This sounds like a major undertaking."* -> "We can start with a single, high-impact use case to demonstrate value quickly and build momentum for a broader transformation."

### 3.3. Playbook: Developers & Agencies

*   **Focus:** Technical superiority, developer experience, and building high-value solutions for clients.
*   **Discovery Questions:**
    *   "What are the biggest frustrations you have with your current development stack?"
    *   "What kind of applications are your clients asking for that are difficult to build with your existing tools?"
    *   "How much time do you spend on boilerplate infrastructure versus creating unique value?"
*   **Objection Handling:**
    *   *"I'm not familiar with this architecture."* -> "We provide comprehensive documentation, a developer portal, and a dedicated support team to get you up to speed quickly."
    *   *"How does this compare to [specific framework]?"* -> "Let's do a technical deep dive. We can show you how our event-driven, composable architecture provides a level of resilience and scalability that other frameworks can't match."
