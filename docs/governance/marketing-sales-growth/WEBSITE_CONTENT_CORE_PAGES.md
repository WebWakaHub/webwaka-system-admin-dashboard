## 1. Website Goal

The primary goal of the WebWaka website is to educate our target audience about the platform, establish our brand as a thought leader in the African tech ecosystem, and generate qualified leads for our sales team and partner program.

## 2. Core Pages & Content Strategy

### 2.1. Homepage

*   **Headline:** Build Your Ecosystem on Africa's Digital Operating System.
*   **Sub-headline:** WebWaka provides the foundational primitives for you to build, launch, and scale resilient, AI-native transactional platforms.
*   **Call to Action (CTA):** "Start Building" (links to a contact form/demo request) and "Explore the Platform" (links to the platform overview page).
*   **Content:** High-level overview of the platform, key benefits, social proof (logos of partners, testimonials), and a section on "Who We Serve."

### 2.2. Platform Overview

*   **Headline:** The Platform for Platforms.
*   **Content:** A detailed explanation of the WebWaka architecture (Layers 1-4), the recursive platform design, and the core primitives. This page should be visually rich with diagrams and infographics.
*   **CTA:** "View a Demo" and "Read the Documentation."

### 2.3. Solutions (by Suite)

*   **Structure:** A main "Solutions" page that links to individual pages for each suite (e.g., Commerce, Transportation).
*   **Content:** Each suite page will detail the specific problems it solves, the features it includes, and use cases relevant to that industry.
*   **CTA:** "Get Started with [Suite Name]" and "Talk to a Specialist."

### 2.4. For Developers

*   **Headline:** Build the Future of African Tech.
*   **Content:** Information on our SDKs, APIs, and developer documentation. This page will highlight the extensibility of the platform and the benefits of building on WebWaka.
*   **CTA:** "Access the Developer Portal" and "Join our Developer Community."

### 2.5. Pricing

*   **Headline:** A Model That Scales With You.
*   **Content:** A clear and transparent overview of our pricing model, including the different tiers, what's included in each, and the logic behind our usage-based pricing. This will be based on the `SUBSCRIPTION_PRICING_STRATEGY.md` document.
*   **CTA:** "Calculate Your Costs" (interactive calculator) and "Contact Sales."

### 2.6. About Us

*   **Headline:** Our Mission: To Empower Africa's Innovators.
*   **Content:** The WebWaka story, our vision for the future of the continent, and profiles of our leadership team.
*   **CTA:** "Join Our Team" (links to a careers page).

### 2.7. Blog/Resources

*   **Headline:** Insights from the Forefront of African Tech.
*   **Content:** A regularly updated blog with articles, case studies, white papers, and webinars on topics relevant to our target audience.
*   **CTA:** "Subscribe to Our Newsletter."

## 3. SEO Strategy

*   **Core Keywords:** "platform as a service Africa," "digital operating system Africa," "headless commerce Africa," "transportation management system Africa."
*   **Content Strategy:** Create high-quality, long-form content that targets these keywords and establishes our authority in the space.
