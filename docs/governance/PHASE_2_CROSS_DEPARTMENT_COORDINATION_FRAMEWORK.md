## Escalation Procedures

### 4-Level Escalation Framework

#### Level 1: Department-Level Resolution (0-24 hours)
**Trigger:** Blocker or conflict arises within department scope  
**Action:** Agent attempts to resolve within their department  
**Documentation:** Document blocker in agent checklist and Master Control Board  
**Escalation Criteria:** If unresolved after 24 hours, escalate to Level 2

**Example Scenarios:**
- Technical implementation questions
- Resource allocation within department
- Timeline adjustments within department scope

---

#### Level 2: Chief of Staff Intervention (24-48 hours)
**Trigger:** Blocker unresolved after 24 hours OR cross-department conflict  
**Action:** Chief of Staff (webwakaagent1) mediates and coordinates resolution  
**Documentation:** Document intervention and resolution in Master Control Board  
**Escalation Criteria:** If unresolved after 48 hours, escalate to Level 3

**Example Scenarios:**
- Cross-department coordination conflicts
- Integration point disagreements
- Resource allocation across departments
- Timeline conflicts between departments

**Chief of Staff Authority:**
- Coordinate resolution between affected departments
- Clarify authority boundaries per FOUNDER_DELEGATION_MATRIX.md
- Propose compromise solutions within governance rules
- Document resolution for future reference

---

#### Level 3: Founder Agent Escalation (48-72 hours)
**Trigger:** Blocker unresolved after 48 hours OR authority boundary question  
**Action:** Chief of Staff escalates to Founder Agent (webwaka007)  
**Documentation:** Provide full context with 4-part review requirement  
**Escalation Criteria:** If unresolved after 72 hours, escalate to Level 4

**Example Scenarios:**
- Strategic decisions beyond agent authority
- Architectural decisions requiring Founder Agent approval
- Governance exceptions or interpretations
- Resource allocation requiring higher authority

**Founder Agent Authority:**
- Make binding decisions within delegated authority
- Approve governance exceptions
- Resolve authority boundary questions
- Escalate to Human Founder if needed

---

#### Level 4: Human Founder Escalation (72+ hours)
**Trigger:** Blocker unresolved after 72 hours OR strategic decision required  
**Action:** Founder Agent escalates to Human Founder  
**Documentation:** Comprehensive escalation report with all context  
**Resolution:** Human Founder makes final binding decision

**Example Scenarios:**
- Strategic direction changes
- Major resource allocation decisions
- Governance framework changes
- Authority boundary expansions

**Human Founder Authority:**
- Ultimate decision authority
- May override any decision
- May modify governance framework
- May adjust Phase 2 scope or timeline

## Decision-Making Protocols

### Decision Authority Matrix

| Decision Type | Authority | Escalation Path |
|--------------|-----------|-----------------|
| Department-level implementation | Department agent | Chief of Staff → Founder Agent |
| Cross-department coordination | Chief of Staff | Founder Agent → Human Founder |
| Architecture decisions | webwakaagent3 | Chief of Staff → Founder Agent |
| Security decisions | webwakaagent5 | Chief of Staff → Founder Agent |
| Product decisions | webwakaagent2 | Chief of Staff → Founder Agent |
| Strategic decisions | Founder Agent | Human Founder |
| Governance decisions | Founder Agent | Human Founder |

### Decision Documentation Requirements

**All decisions must be documented with:**
1. **Context:** What decision is being made and why
2. **Options:** What alternatives were considered
3. **Rationale:** Why this decision was chosen
4. **Authority:** Who made the decision and under what authority
5. **Impact:** What departments/agents are affected
6. **Timeline:** When the decision takes effect

**Documentation Location:** GitHub Issues + Master Control Board
