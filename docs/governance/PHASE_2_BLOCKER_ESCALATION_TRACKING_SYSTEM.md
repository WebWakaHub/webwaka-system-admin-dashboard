## Blocker Tracking Framework

### Blocker Definition

A **blocker** is any issue that:
1. Prevents an agent from completing assigned work
2. Delays a milestone or deliverable
3. Requires cross-department coordination to resolve
4. Requires authority beyond the agent's scope
5. Has been unresolved for >24 hours

### Blocker Categories

| Category | Description | Typical Resolution Time | Escalation Threshold |
|----------|-------------|------------------------|---------------------|
| **Technical** | Technical implementation issues | 24-48 hours | 48 hours |
| **Coordination** | Cross-department coordination needs | 12-24 hours | 24 hours |
| **Resource** | Resource allocation or availability | 24-48 hours | 48 hours |
| **Authority** | Decision requiring higher authority | 12-24 hours | 24 hours |
| **External** | External dependency or vendor issue | 48-72 hours | 72 hours |

## Escalation Procedures

### Level 1: Department-Level Resolution (0-24 hours)

**Trigger:** Blocker arises within department scope  
**Action:** Agent attempts to resolve within their department  
**Documentation:** Document blocker in this tracking system and agent checklist  
**Escalation Criteria:** If unresolved after 24 hours, escalate to Level 2

**Responsibilities:**
- **Agent:** Identify blocker, attempt resolution, document in checklist
- **Chief of Staff:** Monitor blocker age, provide guidance if requested

**Documentation Requirements:**
- Blocker ID, category, severity, description
- Impact assessment and affected agents
- Mitigation actions attempted
- Resolution target date

---

### Level 2: Chief of Staff Intervention (24-48 hours)

**Trigger:** Blocker unresolved after 24 hours OR cross-department conflict  
**Action:** Chief of Staff (webwakaagent1) mediates and coordinates resolution  
**Documentation:** Document intervention and resolution in this tracking system  
**Escalation Criteria:** If unresolved after 48 hours, escalate to Level 3

**Responsibilities:**
- **Chief of Staff:** Coordinate resolution between affected agents
- **Affected Agents:** Participate in resolution discussions
- **Chief of Staff:** Document resolution approach and timeline

**Resolution Approaches:**
- Coordinate cross-department meetings
- Clarify authority boundaries per FOUNDER_DELEGATION_MATRIX.md
- Propose compromise solutions within governance rules
- Reallocate resources if within authority

---

### Level 3: Founder Agent Escalation (48-72 hours)

**Trigger:** Blocker unresolved after 48 hours OR authority boundary question  
**Action:** Chief of Staff escalates to Founder Agent (webwaka007)  
**Documentation:** Provide full context with 4-part review requirement  
**Escalation Criteria:** If unresolved after 72 hours, escalate to Level 4

**Responsibilities:**
- **Chief of Staff:** Prepare comprehensive escalation report
- **Founder Agent:** Review context and make binding decision
- **Chief of Staff:** Document decision and communicate to affected agents

**Escalation Report Requirements:**
1. **What was done:** All resolution attempts and their outcomes
2. **Why it was done:** Rationale for each resolution approach
3. **What is being reviewed:** Specific decision or action needed
4. **What outcome is expected:** Desired resolution and timeline

---

### Level 4: Human Founder Escalation (72+ hours)

**Trigger:** Blocker unresolved after 72 hours OR strategic decision required  
**Action:** Founder Agent escalates to Human Founder  
**Documentation:** Comprehensive escalation report with all context  
**Resolution:** Human Founder makes final binding decision

**Responsibilities:**
- **Founder Agent:** Prepare comprehensive escalation report
- **Human Founder:** Review context and make final binding decision
- **Founder Agent:** Document decision and communicate to all agents

## Blocker Reporting Procedures

### How to Report a Blocker

**Step 1: Identify the Blocker**
- Determine if the issue meets blocker criteria
- Categorize the blocker (Technical, Coordination, Resource, Authority, External)
- Assess severity (Critical, High, Medium, Low)

**Step 2: Document the Blocker**
- Create entry in this tracking system (via Chief of Staff)
- Document in your agent checklist
- Create GitHub Issue with "blocker" label

**Step 3: Attempt Resolution**
- Try to resolve within your department (Level 1)
- Document all resolution attempts
- Update blocker status daily

**Step 4: Escalate if Needed**
- Escalate to Chief of Staff after 24 hours if unresolved
- Provide full context and impact assessment
- Participate in resolution discussions
