## 1. Constitutional Authority & Amendment Notice

This document, version 2.0.0, is ratified by Founder Authority under the **CSRE-01A (Canonical Structure Ratification Amendment) Protocol**. It repeals and supersedes all prior versions of the CSRE-01 constitution.

This amendment was necessitated by the findings of the `CSRE-STRUCT-IDENT-01` audit, which revealed:
1.  A structural mutation event (the addition of 14 AI-native structures) that occurred post-certification without a corresponding constitutional amendment.
2.  A critical error in the CINV-02 reconstruction of CSRE-01 v1.0.0, which erroneously declared 156 structures.

This version corrects the constitutional record to reflect the ground truth of the platform's structure.

## 5. Historical Ratification Clause

This amendment certifies that the 128-structure baseline, as documented in the `WEBWAKA_MASTER_DOCUMENT` and certified by the `GSFVA-01B` audit, was the correct and valid constitutional reality at the time of certification. The subsequent expansion to 142 structures was a valid structural mutation that occurred to support the AI Cognitive Fabric. This amendment formally closes the ratification gap created by that expansion.

This audit confirms that no structures were deleted or merged. The discrepancy was a result of addition and a subsequent reconstruction error.

## 6. Structural Mutation Discipline Clause

Any future addition, removal, or reclassification of a Canonical Structure is a constitutional-level event. Such a mutation is prohibited without a formal amendment protocol that includes, at minimum:

1.  A **Constitutional Amendment Proposal** (e.g., CSRE-01B).
2.  **Founder Ratification** of the proposal.
3.  A **Census Audit** to verify the new structural reality.
4.  An update to the **`WEBWAKA_MASTER_DOCUMENT`**.
5.  Regeneration of the **`MASTER_IMPLEMENTATION_TRACKER`**.
