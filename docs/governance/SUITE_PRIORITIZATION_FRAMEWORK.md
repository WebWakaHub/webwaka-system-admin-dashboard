## 1. Introduction

This document outlines the framework for prioritizing the implementation of the WebWaka Suites. The framework is designed to be transparent, objective, and aligned with the strategic goals of the platform.

## 2. Prioritization Criteria

The following criteria will be used to prioritize the suites:

| Criteria | Weight |
|---|---|
| **Strategic Importance** | 30% |
| **Market Demand** | 25% |
| **Revenue Potential** | 20% |
| **Implementation Complexity** | 15% |
| **Dependencies** | 10% |

### 2.1. Strategic Importance

This criterion assesses the alignment of the suite with the overall strategic goals of the WebWaka platform. Suites that are critical to achieving the platform's vision and mission will be given a higher priority.

### 2.2. Market Demand

This criterion assesses the level of demand for the suite in the target market. Suites that address a clear and pressing need in the market will be given a higher priority.

### 2.3. Revenue Potential

This criterion assesses the potential for the suite to generate revenue for the WebWaka platform. Suites with a clear and viable business model will be given a higher priority.

### 2.4. Implementation Complexity

This criterion assesses the level of effort and resources required to implement the suite. Suites that are less complex to implement will be given a higher priority, as they can be delivered to the market more quickly.

### 2.5. Dependencies

This criterion assesses the dependencies of the suite on other suites or shared primitives. Suites with fewer dependencies will be given a higher priority, as they can be implemented more independently.

## 3. Prioritization Process

The prioritization process will be as follows:

1.  **Scoring:** Each suite will be scored against the prioritization criteria.
2.  **Weighting:** The scores will be weighted according to the assigned weights.
3.  **Ranking:** The suites will be ranked based on their weighted scores.
4.  **Review:** The ranking will be reviewed by the Founder Agent and the Human Founder.
5.  **Finalization:** The final prioritization will be approved by the Human Founder.
