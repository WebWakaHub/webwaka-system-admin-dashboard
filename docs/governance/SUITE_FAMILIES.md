## 1. Introduction

This document formally defines the suite groupings (families) for the WebWaka platform. These groupings provide a logical structure for organizing the suites and will guide future development and team organization.

## 2. Suite Families

The following suite families have been defined:

### 2.1. Business & Marketing

*   **Description:** Suites focused on business operations, marketing, and sales.
*   **Suites:**
    *   Sites & Funnels
    *   Real Estate
    *   Recruitment

### 2.2. Community & Engagement

*   **Description:** Suites focused on community building, engagement, and communication.
*   **Suites:**
    *   Politics
    *   Church
    *   Community
    *   Civic

### 2.3. Transportation & Logistics

*   **Description:** Suites focused on transportation, logistics, and supply chain management.
*   **Suites:**
    *   Logistics
    *   Ride-Hailing
    *   Ridesharing

### 2.4. Service & Operations Management

*   **Description:** Suites focused on service delivery and operations management.
*   **Suites:**
    *   Hospitality
    *   Health
    *   Education
    *   Project Management
